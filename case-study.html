Fintech Dashboard

Revolutionizing mobile banking for 2 million users.

The Challenge

Our client, a leading legacy bank, was struggling with an outdated mobile app that had a 2.5-star rating on the App Store. Users complained about slow load times, confusing navigation, and frequent crashes during high-traffic periods. They needed a complete overhaul to compete with digital-first neobanks.

The Solution

We engaged in a 6-month digital transformation. We started with specialized user research to understand pain points. Our engineering team rebuilt the architecture using a microservices approach to ensure scalability. The UI was redesigned with a focus on "Glassmorphism" to give it a modern, premium feel.

Key Results

  • App Store rating increased to 4.8 stars
  • 40% reduction in server costs
  • User retention improved by 25%
